Why this page?

Reading books brings education.
I really believe that self litteracy is an important thing I would like to share the books I read.
LinkedIn doesn't allow to display the books read ( I guess it's because nobody can really check if you really read or not the book ( except if you use Kindle)).
I took the habbit to read in public transportation rather than playing and I switched a couple year ago from novels, geopolitical analysis and history books to more practical and Resume-valuable books.

My inspiration is the concept of "My Personal MBA", that encourage people to acquire a low budget MBA by reading books. I picked some books from the reading list and complete it with others.

So what? What's the point to list those books?
Obviously, it's not for bragging about the number of books i've read, acquiring knowledge without using them has no value... You can find many summaries& analysis on the internet... Indeed, The internet is a very noisy place, where everyone like to give it opinions and there is no point to paraphrase a book...

The real purpose of this list is to connect more profoundly to people and to grow.

I think that if people knows more, it will help to build deeper and more interesting connection. People will advise me better and give me fresh ideas quicker ( and reference).
